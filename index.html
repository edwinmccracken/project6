Rory McIlroy Driving Analysis

Rory McIlroy and Dustin Johnson have shared the top spot on tour multiple times and are always in the top 5-10 players. Kevin Na on the other hand has never been in that group. The main difference between Kevin Na and Dustin Johnson/Rory is the distance that they hit the ball.
While Kevin Na is a great player and has won tournaments, he has only won 4 times, and has never won a major. In contrast Rory and Dustin Johnson have combined for 40 wins.
